About Me

Experience

Having been brought up in a building family, I have learned much from my father and grandfather from an early age. Over my 20 year career, I have worked on a vast variety of projects both under direction and as the director. This wealth of experience ensures that I am handle familiar challenges quickly and efficiently, and new challenges confidently and creatively.

I value the long-term relationships that I form with my clients, which are built upon honest evaluations, reliable service, good outcomes and sensible pricing.
